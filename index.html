Empowering Girls To Code

What Is Coding?

Think of coding as having a dog. You can train your dog to do cool tricks with clear instructions. The more information you give your dog, the smarter it gets. Coding is all about giving instructions to a device in order for it to accomplish a specific task. Technology is a huge part of our present and future. You wouldn't be reading this right now if it wasn't for HTML and CSS code. Learning to code will unlock endless possibilities!

Why Should You Learn To Code?

Only 18% of the Computer Science field is made up of women. Women working in Computer Science earn 94% of what men earn. While there is still a significant gender pay gap in the field, Computer Science is one of the fields with the smallest gender pay gaps.

The best part is that it's never too late to start coding. Anyone can learn at any time, regardless of how much experience you have.

While the percentage of the Computer Science field consisting of women was increasing for a while, in recent years, the percentage started decreasing. You can help change that.
